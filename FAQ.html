General Questions

The Galaxy S26 Ultra introduces revolutionary features including:

  • Advanced modular system with interchangeable components
  • Under-display camera technology for a truly bezel-less display
  • Next-generation AI processor with dedicated neural processing unit
  • 100W fast charging capability
  • 6G connectivity support
  • Enhanced S-Pen with reduced latency and new gesture controls

The Samsung Galaxy S26 Ultra is scheduled for release in March 2025. Pre-orders will begin in February 2025 with exclusive early-bird benefits for those who pre-order.

The Galaxy S26 Ultra will be available in the following premium finishes:

  • Phantom Black
  • Cosmic Silver
  • Emerald Green
  • Burgundy Red
  • Sky Blue (Exclusive online color)

Yes, the Galaxy S26 Ultra features an IP68 rating, meaning it is dust-tight and can be submerged in up to 1.5 meters of freshwater for up to 30 minutes. However, this rating does not apply when using modular attachments.

Specifications Questions

The Galaxy S26 Ultra features a 5000mAh intelligent battery with AI-powered optimization. With the optional Power-Mod attachment, you can extend this to 9700mAh for all-day heavy usage without needing to recharge.

The Galaxy S26 Ultra is powered by the next-generation Snapdragon 8 Gen 4 processor with an integrated AI Neural Processing Unit. This provides up to 40% better performance and 35% improved power efficiency compared to previous generations.

The Galaxy S26 Ultra features a 6.8-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display with:

  • 3200 x 1440 resolution (QHD+)
  • 120Hz adaptive refresh rate (1-120Hz)
  • 240Hz touch sampling rate
  • 2500 nits peak brightness
  • Under-display front camera technology
  • Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 3 protection

Modular Features Questions

The Galaxy S26 Ultra features a revolutionary magnetic modular system that allows you to attach various functional modules to enhance your phone's capabilities. Modules simply snap onto the back of the device and connect via a proprietary high-speed data and power interface.

Available modules include:

  • Power-Mod (4700mAh additional battery)
  • Camera-Mod (enhanced zoom and sensor capabilities)
  • Printer-Mod (instant photo printing)
  • Security-Mod (hardware encryption)
  • Satellite-Mod (global connectivity)
  • VR-Mod (virtual reality optimization)

Yes, all modular attachments are sold separately. However, pre-order bundles may include one module of your choice at a discounted price. Individual modules will be available for purchase through Samsung's official website and authorized retailers.

No, the IP68 water and dust resistance rating only applies to the phone itself without any modules attached. When using modular attachments, we recommend avoiding exposure to water or dust to prevent damage to the connection interface.
