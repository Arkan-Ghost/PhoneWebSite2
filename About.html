Our Journey

1938

Foundation

Samsung was founded by Lee Byung-chul as a trading company in Su-dong, South Korea.

1969

Entering Electronics

Samsung-Sanyo Electronics was established, marking Samsung's entry into the electronics industry.

1988

Global Expansion

Samsung Electronics merged with Samsung Semiconductor & Communications, expanding globally.

2000

Mobile Innovation

Samsung became one of the world's largest mobile phone manufacturers, pioneering new technologies.

2010

Galaxy Series

The Samsung Galaxy S series launched, revolutionizing the smartphone market with innovative features.

2025

Galaxy S26 Ultra

Introducing the most advanced smartphone yet - the Galaxy S26 Ultra with groundbreaking AI and modular capabilities.
